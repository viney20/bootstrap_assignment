PIZZA

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 package (1/4 ounce) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons chopped garlic
  • 1 teaspoon
  • 1-1/4 cups warm water (110° to 115°)
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 pound ground beef

METHODS

  1. Heat the oil in a wok over medium heat. Saute garlic and green chile for 30 seconds, then stir in onions and ginger.
  2. Put the flour into a large bowl, then stir in the yeast and salt. Make a well, pour in 200ml warm water and the olive oil and bring together with a wooden spoon until you have a soft, fairly wet dough.
  3. Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base.
  4. Cook until onions are brown. Add tomatoes, and cook until pasty. Stir in cauliflower, cabbage, peas, carrots and potatoes.

PAV BHAJI

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons chopped garlic
  • 1 teaspoon
  • finely chopped green chile peppers
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 cup chopped roma (plum) tomatoes
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 3 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped green chile peppers

METHODS

  1. Heat the oil in a wok over medium heat. Saute garlic and green chile for 30 seconds, then stir in onions and ginger.
  2. Cook until onions are brown. Add tomatoes, and cook until pasty. Stir in cauliflower, cabbage, peas, carrots and potatoes.
  3. Season with pav bhaji masala. Cover, and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.Season with salt,and stir in lemon juice.
  4. Toast the dinner rolls, and spread lightly with butter. Serve garnished with chopped onion, green chile and cilantro.

CHOWMEIN

INGRIDIENTS

  • 200 Gram Fresh noodles
  • 5 Cups Water
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Chilli powder
  • 1/4 Cup Onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp Soy sauce

METHODS

  1. heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. add chopped onions or spring onions.
  3. add sliced mushrooms and finely chopped french beans.
  4. stir and saute on a medium flame till the edges of the mushroom get lightly browned.
  5. add grated carrots, shredded cabbage and sliced bell pepper/capsicum.
  6. Now add salt, black pepper powder, tomato sauce, chilli sauce, soy sauce and vinegar to veggie mixture. Mix well.
  7. Garnish with red capsicum and pour the prepared green-chilli vinegar mix on top.
